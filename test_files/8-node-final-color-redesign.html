Recommendation: Scheme 1 provides the best spectrum distribution with Storage as a proper neutral gray. The colors are semantically appropriate and provide excellent visual distinction.

  • Red Persona - Human-focused, warm
  • Orange Interface - Digital interaction, vibrant
  • Yellow Process - Active workflow, energy
  • Green Capability - Growth, power, potential
  • Teal Outcome - Success, achievement, results
  • Blue Resource - Reliability, assets, materials
  • Purple Knowledge - Wisdom, intellect, information
  • Gray Storage - Neutral infrastructure, utility
